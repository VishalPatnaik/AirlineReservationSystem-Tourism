Cognitive Disabilities

Please let us know when a guest with a cognitive intellectual disability is traveling who may require assistance. As these types of disabilities may not be readily apparent to our teammates, communication of needs should be made in a timely manner. 

This includes any guest, because of a mental disability, cannot understand or respond appropriately to safety instructions from Vol En Avion teammates.

Pre-boarding is available for guests with intellectual/cognitive disabilities. We also offer appropriate seating accommodations when requested. Please make us aware of the assistance you need as far in advance of your travel as possible.

Please note: Vol En Avion will not offer assistance with feeding, administering medication or inside airport restrooms or lavatories on board the aircraft. If this type of assistance is needed a personal care attendant will be required to travel with the guest.
