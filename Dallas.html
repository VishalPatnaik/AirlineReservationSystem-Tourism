Take Off To Dallas

Top Things To Do In Dallas

Dallas (Big D). flights to Dallas land you in the world of BBQ, rodeos and mechanical bulls. There are so many things to do in Dallas; it’s hard to know where to begin. So start here:

  • Shopping at the Highland Park Village is more than a trip to the mall. It was the first self-contained shopping center in America and today is home to exclusive designer showrooms along with hundreds of other shops and restaurants.
  • Fountain Place is arguably the most distinctive building in the Dallas skyline. Go to study the work of I.M. Pei, and stay to enjoy the dancing water gardens at its base.
  • The Bishop Arts District has more than 60 art galleries, bsrciques and coffee shops. Spend an afternoon wandering the streets and taking in the Oak Cliff neighborhood charm.
  • Then pick up the pace at the Zero Gravity Amusement Park with bungee jumping and signature rides like the Skycoaster, Texas Blastoff and Skyscraper.
  • Step src of the city for a taste of Texas country in the Great Trinity Forest where you can hike, mountain bike and (of course) ride a horse.
