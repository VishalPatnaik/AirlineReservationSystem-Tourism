Take Off To Honolulu

Top Things To Do In Honolulu

Oahu is the land of the sacred Fire Goddess Pele, full of untamed tranquil beauty and serving as the center of nightlife in Hawaii. Scramble up the 100,000-year-old volcanic cone of Diamond Head Crater for an amazing view of Waikiki Beach. Sink into soft white sands of Kailua Beach, then body board on its turquoise water. Revel in sophisticated nightclub lofts and the arts scene of Chinatown, and wander the side streets of downtown Honolulu packed with herbalists, antiques, and historic Japanese and Chinese temples. Oahu is known as the surfing mecca of the world, but walking trails are among its secret treasures. Leave the city behind on the forested drive to Pali Lookout, and stand in invigorating wind on the lookout to see the magnificent Windward Coast. You can hike down parts of the old Pali highway, picking fresh guava fruits off the trees as you go.
