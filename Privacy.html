Privacy Policy

General

When you communicate with Vol En Avion, our reservation service providers, or our loyalty program, Elevate (collectively, “Vol En Avion”), we collect information about you to help us provide and improve our services, websites, mobile application, features and content, and to administer our relationship with you (together, the “Service”). In this Privacy Policy, we describe the sources and types of information we collect, how we use it, when it may be shared with others, and how we reasonably attempt to safeguard its confidentiality and security. This Privacy Policy represents our policies and intended business practices; it is not a contract and does not create any legal rights or obligations.

What We Collect And How We Collect It

Personally Identifiable Information

Some of the information we collect on our Sites, IFE, App, through social networking services, and in promotional activities can be used to contact or identify you or is maintained in personally identifiable form (“Personal Information”).  The Personal Information we may receive includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name, physical addresses, e-mail addresses, and telephone numbers;
  • Date of birth and gender;
  • Passport number, Known Traveler number, or redress number;
  • Credit card numbers, and associated billing information;
  • Travel related information such as flight details, travel companions, emergency contacts, seating preferences, dietary requests, and medical needs;
  • Transactional and online activity data related to points earned through Elevate partners;
  • Corporate-contract, employer, or other affiliation (such as employer name, title, and contract information);
  • Other personal information you may voluntarily provide to customer-service representatives or via your participation in surveys or focus groups.

We also receive information from you that is not itself Personal Information (e.g., zip code or individual preferences) (“Non-Identifying Information”). Certain Non-Identifying Information will be considered a part of your Personal Information if it is combined with other identifiers in a way that identifies you (e.g., combining your zip code with your street address). For the avoidance of doubt, the same pieces of information are considered Non-Identifying Information when they are taken alone or combined only with other Non-Identifying Information.

User provided data will be stored and accessible if you create an Elevate account, you sign into your Elevate account through the Site or App or make purchases, pay fees, or make other arrangements as a guest. If this data is stored in your Elevate account, it will not be accessible by the Site or App if you log out of your account or choose to delete this information.

Passive Data Collection

When you visit the Sites, whether as a registered user or an unregistered browser (either of these, a “Vol En Avion User”), our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website (“Log Data”). This log data includes, but is not limited to, information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type or the website from which you are visiting, pages of our site that you visit, time spent on those pages, information you search for, access times and dates, and other statistics. We may use this information to monitor and analyze use of the Sites and the Service, increase the Sites’ functionality, security, and user-friendliness, and better tailor the Sites to our visitors’ needs. For example, some of this information is collected so that when you visit the Sites again, it will recognize you, and serve advertisements and other information appropriate to you and your interests. We also use this information to verify that visitors to the Site meet the criteria required to process their requests.

The App may collect certain information from you automatically in order to improve your experience and maximize functionality. This information may include but is not limited to your domain name, what App features you use, transaction history and details, amount of time you use the App, your device identification, data concerning your operating system and/or browser, and App links you click.

Cookies

Like many websites, we use “cookies” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer’s hard disk for record-keeping purposes. We use persistent cookies to save your registration ID and login password for future logins to the Sites. We also utilize session ID cookies to enable certain features of the Sites, to better understand how you interact with the Sites, and to monitor aggregate usage by Vol En Avion Users and web traffic routing on the Sites.

Third party advertisers on the Sites may also place or read cookies on your browser. You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from websites you visit. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use all portions of the Sites or all functionality of the Service.

App Notifications

When opening the mobile app for the first time a pop-up window will give you the option to receive push notifications from Vol En Avion about Tourist Guide updates, gate changes, or special offers.  You will also have the option to enable or disable these notifications through the App Settings menu. You can also enable text message notifications through your Elevate account or when purchasing tickets. Please consult your mobile carrier plan as standard text messaging and data rates will apply.

How Your Information Is Used

We use your Personal Information (in some cases, in conjunction with your Non-Identifying Information) to provide the Service, complete your transactions, administer your inquiries, and to contact you with Vol En Avion e-mails, updates, promotional materials, and other information that may be of interest to you.

We may combine your Personal Information with Non-Identifying Information and aggregate it with information collected from other users to analyze how our Sites and Service are used in order to provide you with a better experience. We may also use it combined, but not aggregated, to serve you specifically (e.g., to deliver a meal to you according to your dietary requests).

Who Your Information Is Shared With

We may employ third party companies and individuals to provide or assist in providing the services or products you request, perform Site-related services (e.g., maintenance services, Web analytics, and database management), or for other businesses purposes. These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks and are contractually obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. We may also disclose Personal Information concerning your travel to third parties, such as employers, who have arranged for discounts or purchased travel or other services on your behalf.

We share your Personal Information with Vol En Avion partners and affiliates for the purpose of providing you with services, information, or promotional opportunities that may be of interest to you. 

We may share aggregated information such as demographic information, domain names, other site traffic information, etc., with third parties for industry analysis, demographic profiling, and other purposes. Any aggregated information shared in these contexts will not contain your Personal Information.

Registered Users

When you register through the Sites, interact with the Sites, App or our in-flight entertainment (IFE) system, or communicate with other users, Vol En Avion Users will see your “Screen Name” and sometimes the month and year you first registered with Vol En Avion (“Required Identifiers”). You can choose what other Personal Information you provide as part of your profile (“Other Registered User Information”), and, in some cases, you can choose whether to share that information publicly on the Sites by making selections via the “user preferences” section of your profile webpage. Providing Other Registered User Information is voluntary. To help guard your anonymity and sensitive information, we recommend not using your real name as your “Screen Name.”

Social Networking

When you connect with Vol En Avion through a forum, chat room, or other social networking service (including, without limitation, Twitter, Facebook, or Instagram), any Personal Information you share may be visible to other users and can be read, collected, or used by them or us. You are responsible for the  information you choose to submit in these instances and are encouraged to read the privacy policies or statements of these social networking sites.

Compliance with Laws and Law Enforcement

Vol En Avion cooperates with government and law enforcement officials and private parties to enforce and comply with the law. Personal Information may be collected, used, processed, and transferred to facilitate travel, including obtaining immigration and customs clearance, and to comply with, or assist in the development of, security or safety measures for passengers, baggage, or cargo.

We will disclose your Personal Information to government authorities or private parties pursuant to subpoenas or other legal process. We may also use or disclose your Personal Information as permitted by law to protect the property and rights of Vol En Avion, our customers, our websites and its users, for fraud detection or prevention, for the prevention, detection, apprehension or prosecution of criminal acts, or when we, in our sole discretion, believe that disclosure is in the interest of aviation security or otherwise necessary or advisable.

Business Transfers

In the event Vol En Avion is part of a business transaction with another company, such as a merger, corporate reorganization, acquisition, the sale of all or a portion of our assets, or in the event of bankruptcy, information that we have collected from or about you or your devices may be disclosed to such other entity as part of the due diligence or business integration process and may be included in any related transfer of assets.

We do not and will not, at any time, request your credit card information, your account ID, login password, or national identification numbers in an unsolicited e-mail or telephone communication. For more information about phishing, visit the Federal Trade Commission’s website.

Our Policy Toward Children

Our Sites and App are not directed to children under 18. We will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 without prior parental consent. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with Personal Information without their consent, he or she should contact Vol En Avion Guest Relations and we will delete such information from our files. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Personal Information of minors will be retained and used  to the extent necessary to provide the Service and to comply with applicable laws, including federal aviation or security regulations.
