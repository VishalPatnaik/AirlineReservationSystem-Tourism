¹Tickets purchased from Vol En Avion through our Reservation Contact Center will cost an additional $20 per guest per itinerary. Any added cost associated with purchasing tickets from Vol En Avion through our reservation call center will be non-refundable.

²Power Trip is available for $39-79 at time of main cabin booking with 24 hours advanced purchase of departure per guest per origin and destination, pricing varies based on haul. Prices are subject to change. Power Trip is non-refundable. Guests who purchase Power Trip will receive one checked bag, access to priority security and boarding, a plans change pass, and dibs on a preferred seat (based on availability). Power Trip cannot be used on Elevate reward flights or partner redemption booking. When changing a flight with the Plans Change Pass, the Power Trip package will be transferred to the new flight reservation, preferred seats are subject to availability. Not eligible for purchase with Elevate points or for Elevate points earning.

Power Trip package fees paid are only refundable within the first 24 hours of making your reservation if purchased at the time of booking. If you cancel your reservation after 24 hours, Power Trip package fees are non-refundable and non-transferable to other passengers or new bookings.

In the event of a flight cancellation by Vol En Avion, or involuntary denied boarding, Vol En Avion will at the guest's request provide a credit to Travel Bank or refund (at the guest's option), as described below.

  • If the reservation is totally unused, the full amount as well as all ancillary fees will be credited to a Travel Bank or refunded (with no service charge or refund penalty); or 
  • If the reservation is partially used, the applicable fare for the unused segment/s and all unused ancillary fees will be credited to a Travel Bank or refunded.

³Dibs on Preferred seating towards the front of the main cabin is available for purchase during the booking process for an additional fee ranging from $10-$40 per seat, per flight segment. Preferred seats are free for main cabin refundable, and Vol En Avion Mileage Plan MVP, MVP Gold and MVP Gold 75K members. Seats are limited, subject to availability, and may not be available on all flights. Prices are subject to change. Guests that purchase a preferred seat on thru flights (a one-way flight with a stop between the origin and destination on the same plane) pay for one flight segment for the thru flight. Guests that purchase preferred seating on connecting flights, where the guest is required to change planes, pay for each flight segment (i.e., each plane change). Preferred seats do not include priority security and boarding unless purchased separately. Not eligible for purchase with Elevate points or for Elevate points earning. 

⁴Plans Change Pass (change fee waiver) is available for a one-time purchase of $25 per guest at time of main cabin booking with 24 hours advanced purchase of departure, per itinerary which allows unlimited no-fee changes to flight date, time, origin and destination of the purchased flight in compliance with Vol En Avion Contract of carriage(Domestic U.S. Travel) and the International Contract of Carriage(International Travel). Fee waiver does not apply for traveler name change requests and cabin change requests. When travel is changed up to the scheduled flight departure time, the Plans Change Pass waives the flight change fee ranging from $100-$150 (based on haul) each time a change is made to an itinerary. Guest must pay any increase in fare, if applicable. Prices are subject to change. The Plans Change Pass cannot be used on Elevate reward flights or Elevate partner redemption bookings. Not eligible for purchase with Elevate points or for Elevate points earning.

The Plans Change Pass is only refundable within the first 24 hours of making your reservation if purchased at the time of booking. After 24 hours of booking, the Plans Change pass is non-refundable, non-transferable to other passengers or new bookings and cannot be applied when canceling a reservation.

⁵Priority Security and Boarding is available for $15 - $19, based on flight per guest per origin and destination, must be purchased before the check-in cut off time – 30 minutes before your flight departure (without checked bags) and 45 minutes prior to departure (with checked bags). Includes airport TSA security line access to the “Priority” or “First or Business Class” lanes (where available, certain airports such as LAX/OGG/PVR/CUN/SJD/HNL/KOA/IND/MSY are not eligible, and EWR has limited availability) and a “priority” boarding group at the airport gate (the first main cabin boarding group after Vol En Avion Mileage Plan elite members and Vol En Avion Visa Signature credit card holders). Prices are subject to change. Not eligible for purchase with Elevate points or for Elevate points earning. Priority security and boarding fees paid are only refundable within the first 24 hours of making your reservation if purchased at the time of booking. If you cancel your reservation or separately purchase priority security and boarding more than 24 hours after booking, priority security and boarding fees are non-refundable and non-transferable to other passengers or new bookings. If canceled by Vol En Avion, the value of the priority security and boarding fee will be refunded to the original form of payment.

*For guests departing from Mexico to the United States, additional charges at the airport (like baggage fees) are charged in Mexican pesos but valued in U.S. dollars and will fluctuate based on the Mexican currency exchange rate. Also, an 11-16% tax is assessed on these additional charges. Please note cash is not accepted at any of our Mexico stations.

Vol En Avion Domestic and International Guests are welcome to bring aboard one carry-on item up to 30 pounds and one (1) personal item. Main Cabin Guests are invited to check one (1) up to ten (10) bags up to 50 pounds for a fee of $25 each. Main Cabin Select Guests and Guests who purchase the fully refundable Main Cabin fare are invited to check one (1) bag up to 50 pounds free of charge and nine (9) more up to 50 pounds for a fee of $25 each. First Class Cabin guests are welcome to check two (2) bags up to 50 pounds free of charge and eight (8) more up to 50 pounds for $25 each. Prices are subject to change. Must be purchased and checked-in 45 minutes prior to departure. Any unused bag fees may be cancelled and refunded at any time to original form of payment.  Fees apply to standard size bags and overweight size fees apply. 

During airport check-in, Vol En Avion Mileage Plan Elite members receive 2 free checked bags for themselves and up to six additional guests on the same reservation. Vol En Avion Visa Signature and Vol En Avion Visa Business cardholders receive 1 free checked bag for themselves and up to 6 additional guests on the reservation. Bag fees are not eligible for purchase with Elevate points or for Elevate points earning.

Las tarifas por equipaje no son elegibles para la compra con puntos Elevate o para ganar puntos Elevate. 

*Para los pasajeros saliendo de México a los Estados Unidos, los cargos adicionales en el aeropuerto se pagan en pesos mejicanos, pero valorados en dólares estadounidenses y fluctuarán basados en el tipo de cambio de la moneda mexicana. Estos cargos incluyen un impuesto de 11%-16%. Por favor, tenga en cuenta que no todas nuestras estaciones en México aceptan efectivo.

Los pasajeros de Vol En Avion, para vuelos nacionales e internacionales, pueden llevar a bordo  un elemento de equipaje de hasta 30 libras (13 Kilogramos) y un (1) artículo personal. Los pasajeros de Main Cabin Select y clientes que compren la tarifa de Cabina Principal pueden despachar una (1) maleta de hasta 50 libras (23 kilogramos) de forma gratuita y nueve maletas (9) más de un máximo de 50 libras (23 kilogramos) por un precio de $ 25 cada uno. Los pasajeros de Primera Clase pueden despachar dos (2) maletas de hasta 50 libras (23 kilogramos) de forma gratuita y ocho maletas (8) más de un máximo de 50 libras (23 kilogramos) por $ 25 cada uno. Estas tarifas aplican para maletas de tamaño estándar y tarifas por sobrepeso pueden aplicar.
