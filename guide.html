VISHAL

Instead of looking at composition as a set of ‘rules’ to follow – I view it as a set of ingredients that can be taken out of the pantry at any point and used to make a great ‘meal’.
Alternatively I’ve often described it as a set of ‘tools’ that can be taken out of one’s compositional tool belt at any given time in making a great day.
The key is to remember that in the same way as a chef rarely uses all the ingredients at their disposal in any dish – I rarely use all of the ingredients of nature in the making your day more effective and attractive.
